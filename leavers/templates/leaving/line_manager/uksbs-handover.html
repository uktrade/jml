The last step for you to offboard {{ leaver_name }} is to inform payroll by completing the UKSBS form, and update the resulting PDF into our system. Below here you will find guidance and key information to complete this last step.

You need to:

  • Go to the 'My Leavers' section inside the UKSBS home page and select 'UKSBS Leavers form'.
  • Report in the form whether the leaver is paid or unpaid. A leaver is unpaid only when they are a fast streamer or on loan from another department.
  • Report in the form whether the leaver has annual leave remaining and if a payment or a deduction should be made. As their Line Manager, you should have access to this information through UKSBS Connect. More guidance on UKSBS Connect is available here.
