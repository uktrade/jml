DIT Leaving service: what you need to do

At the moment, you can use this service if you are:

  • resigning from the Civil Service (not transferring to a different department)
  • a contractor at the end of your contract

Before you start

To complete the form, you will need to know:

  • your security level
  • the last day you will work at DIT
  • the last date you are employed and paid by DIT (your leaving date)

You must have agreed this information with your line manager before using this service.

The service should take less than 10 minutes to complete.

If you are leaving DIT for a different reason, read the Leaving DIT guidance on Digital Workspace.
