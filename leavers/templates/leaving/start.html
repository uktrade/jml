Leaving DIT: what you need to do

Use this service if you are leaving DIT, as a:

  • civil servant
  • contractor

After you complete and send this form, your line manager will need to confirm some information. They will receive further instructions by email.

Before you start

To complete the form, you will need to know:

  • your security clearance level
  • the last day you will work at DIT
  • the last date you are employed and paid by DIT (your leaving date)

You and your line manager must have agreed on your last working day and leaving date before you can use this service.

This form should take less than 10 minutes to compete.
