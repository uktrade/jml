Leaving DIT: leaver's actions

Whether you are a civil servant or contractor it is mandatory that you complete this form, so all teams that process your off-boarding will receive the information they need to do so.

Your line manager will be notified when you have completed this form as they will be asked to confirm some of the information you have provided.

This form should take less than 10 mins to compete

Before you start

To complete the form, you will need to know your:

  • security clearance level
  • leaving date, the last day you are emploved by DIT
  • last working day, the last day you will be working at DIT

Your leaving date and your last working day should have been agreed with your line manager before using this service, if you haven't done so, please contact your line manager.
