{{ SERVICE_NAME }} - Beta - {{ page_title }}

What it is and why it is needed

The {{ SERVICE_NAME }} is intended to replace the multiple forms and other processes currently used when someone leaves {{ DEPARTMENT_ACRONYM }}.

This means that not only is the experience of leaving {{ DEPARTMENT_ACRONYM }} more efficient and simple to complete, It also ensures that the department has good record keeping on all aspects of leaving.

What it does

The {{ SERVICE_NAME }} allows for contracted or Civil Servant staff to provide information needed by the department for offboarding purposes.

The service automatically notifies the various {{ DEPARTMENT_ACRONYM }} directorates who have responsibility of ensuring that a leaver's access to software, tools and services is removed.

Additionally, it ensures that payroll is updated so that final salaries can be paid promptly.

What it does not do

The service does not provide the ability to officially resign from a post. It assumes that the required agreement for leaving the department has already taken place.

It does not currently cover:

  • loans or temporary loans
  • transfers to other government departments
  • retirement, partial retirement or retirement due to ill health
  • dismissal
  • death in service

How it works

Once you have logged in (no sign up necessary), the service will guide you through the process of providing information relevant to your offboarding. This may include:

  • security clearance level
  • access to Cirrus or Rosa kit
  • additional IT equipment
  • outstanding holiday or flexi time
  • leaving date and last working date
  • correspondence details

By adding or confirming these details, the service can then provide information to the various staff who administer offboarding in {{ DEPARTMENT_ACRONYM }}.

What we would like you to do

Thank you for agreeing to be part of this private beta.

We would like you to use the service to complete the offboarding areas that you are responsible for.

The service itself is an automated service. It will communicate with you at appropriate moments and guide you in how to use it.

Due to the limited number of participants in this private beta, we would be immensely grateful for any and all feedback on your use of the service.

You will notice a feedback form on every page of the service. Please do use it to let us have any feedback, good or bad.
